    • Instructions

    • The object of the game is to create words with the letters in your hand. All words must connect to each other. Money is awarded for each letter played, and longer words with more complex letters earn more money.
    • To look around the scene, hold down the left mouse button and move the mouse. To zoom, simply use the scroll wheel on the mouse.
    • On your turn, you must play a tile by left clicking on the tile. After clicking once, move your mouse to where you want on the gameboard. If the tile flashes green, you may left click again to play the tile. A red flash indicates an invalid move.
    • To remove a tile on your turn, right click on the desired tile. It will go back into your inventory.
    • To access any upgrades or to end your turn, click the arrow on the right side of your screen to view the menu.
